About Our School

Institut Teknologi Del is a private university located in Sitoluama, Toba, North Sumatra. It was founded by General (Ret.) Luhut Binsar Pandjaitan through Del Foundation to enhance skills and welfare in rural communities.

Vision

To become a center of excellence in technology utilization for national advancement.

Mission

  • Deliver superior and sustainable education.
  • Develop and disseminate knowledge and technology.
  • Act as a renewal agent in community development.
  • Implement the Three Pillars of Higher Education.

Study Programs

  • Informatics
  • Information Systems
  • Bioprocess Engineering
  • Metallurgical Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Computer Technology
